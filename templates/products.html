How ARCTIC works

ARCTIC combines thermal imaging technology with intuitive display
interfaces to create a system that:

  • Captures heat signatures through smoke, dust, and darkness
  • Projects critical information directly into the firefighters field of view
  • Highlights temperature variations to identify fire sources and victims
  • Integrates with existing firefighter gear for seamless operation
